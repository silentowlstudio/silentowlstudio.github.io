About Us

SilentOwl Studio is a creative studio located in Depok, West Java, Indonesia. Our services focus on illustration, graphic design, and designer logos. Founded in 2017, Silentowl Studio has worked for many projects and clients from all over the world. They are come from the music industry, creative, printing, clothing company, beverage and food companies, skateboard ,snowboard and others. Producing the logo design, t-shirt design, poster design, skateboard/snowboard graphik, Album/EP design, Character design, Mascot etc.
